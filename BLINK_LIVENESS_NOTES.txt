Blink Liveness Notes

The attendance camera uses blink liveness verification as the mandatory live-person gate.

Workflow:
1. The student looks at the camera.
2. The system observes an open-eye state.
3. The student blinks once slowly.
4. The system detects a closed-eye state.
5. The eyes open again.
6. The liveness state becomes verified for a short period.
7. Captured-face matching and attendance marking are allowed only after verification.

Recommended use:
- Keep the face centered.
- Blink slowly once: open eyes, close eyes, open eyes again.
- Hold the face steady for one or two seconds after blinking.
- Press SPACE if automatic matching is delayed.
